# Wrap Preview

**Professional Contact Sheets & Visual Review Notes for Modern Filmmakers.**

Wrap Preview is a local-first application designed to streamline the review process for high-end cinematic workflows. Organize your scenes, verify technical metadata, and export beautiful production-ready reports without your data ever leaving your machine.

---

## 🔒 Privacy & Data
Your privacy is our priority. Wrap Preview processes all video files and project metadata locally. We do not track you, and we do not store your media on our servers.
