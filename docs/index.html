CineFlow Suite

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

Local-First Commitment

CineFlow Suite is a local-first application built for filmmakers, photographers, and creative directors. We believe your creative work belongs to you, and that privacy is a fundamental requirement for professional workflows.

Our core philosophy is simple: Your data never leaves your computer.

1. Data Collection & Processing

Media Files: CineFlow Suite processes your video and photo files locally on your machine to generate contact sheets, review notes, and technical metadata. We do not upload, transmit, or store your media files on any external servers.

Project Metadata: All project data, including shot lists, ratings, and comments, are stored within a local database on your device. We do not have access to your projects.

Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools (e.g., no cookies, no Google Analytics, no SDKs).

2. Permissions

CineFlow Suite requires specific permissions to function in a sandboxed environment:

  • File System Access: To read your media and save your exports.
  • Local Network: Used strictly for internal communication between application components on your local machine (localhost).

3. Third-Party Integration

CineFlow Suite is self-contained. Any external tools used (like FFmpeg or REDline) are shipped with the application and run locally within your system's security sandbox.
