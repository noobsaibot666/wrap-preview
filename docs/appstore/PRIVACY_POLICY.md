# Privacy Policy for CineFlow Suite

**Effective Date:** April 8, 2026

CineFlow Suite ("the App") is developed with a strict focus on user privacy and data security. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## 1. Local-First Design
CineFlow Suite is a **local-first application**. 
- **Media Files:** The App processes your video and photo files locally on your machine for the purpose of generating contact sheets, review notes, and scene blocks. We **do not upload**, store, or transmit your media files to any external servers.
- **Project Data:** All project metadata, clip ratings, and database information are stored locally in an encrypted or standard SQLite database on your device.

## 2. Permissions
To function correctly on macOS, the App requires the following permissions, which you grant via system prompts:
- **File System Access:** Required to read media files you select and to save exported reports (PDF, PNG, CSV) to your chosen locations.
- **Networking:** The App uses a local loopback network (localhost) for internal communication between UI components. It does not use this connection to transmit data to the internet unless you explicitly trigger an update check.

## 3. Third-Party Services
CineFlow Suite does not include third-party tracking, analytics (like Google Analytics), or advertising SDKs.

## 4. Data Security
Because your data never leaves your computer, the security of your information is protected by your own macOS security settings and disk encryption (FileVault). We recommend keeping your system updated to the latest macOS version.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the App or on our website.
